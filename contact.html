Help Rebuild Sketchdude Studio!

To those of you who want to see more of the kind of work I can do, consider helping me out. here are some things you can do.

You can commission me to do pencil works! It's really almost impossible for me to make any money from oil painting because of the operating expenses- to make even minimum wage, I have to price small oil paintings in the thousands of dollars. I can work fast enough doing pencil drawings to make fairly reasonable income. Send me an email and I will consult with you.

Download my works and print them out! Every time someeone enjoys my work it helps me get the word out that I haven't lost interest or died or something.

Donate! I need art supplies, and computer equipment. For ten dollars amonth I can probably register a domain and buy hosting for a real website. With a decent camera, I could shoot some video of in progress work!
